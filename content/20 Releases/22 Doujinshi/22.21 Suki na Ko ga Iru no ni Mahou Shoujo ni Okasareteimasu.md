## Commentary

A long overdue backlog that's finally done and finished. This was meant to be 3rd? 4th? NSFW doujinshi release, but it got forgotten until last month.

All hail the possesive sister overlord. The yandere aspect isn't as much as the other translated Takei Ooki works, in favor for borderline personality change between her usual self and mahou shoujo one. Not complaining tho, this is nice as well.

One thing to note, if you haven't noticed yet, is that the little sister normally calls her brother *"Onii-chan"*, and calls him *"Onii-san"* while being a mahou shoujo. Dissociation much? Might also be the fact that she doesn't want her brother to know that it was his sister that raped him, though.

Next translation will be the first part of Ardan x Chiyono duology, and a commissioned translation.
