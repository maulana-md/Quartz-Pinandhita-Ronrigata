## Commentary

This translation was commissioned by Asyatolfo.

Good shit from Uno Ryoku. Really, REALLY HOT blowjob scene.

![[coom.jpg]]

This also marks the second Uno Ryoku doujinshi we worked on, the first being [[22.07 Mika to H Suru Hon]]. The contents are simple but very effective; "Supplementary" lessons are a staple of Japanese erotica, after all, swinging on all spectrums of fetishes and preferences. Simple prompts like this are Uno Ryoku's aces, I think, focusing more on what would build the sex scenes and writing only necessary, but still present, plot, if only something so simple.

On a more personal note, I feel that this is my peak typesetting career. Really satisfied with how the moans and sfx came out. I hope it did the doujinshi justice.

I missed adding the translated title on the exh gallery. The appropriate translation would be ***After School Special Supplementary Lesson***.
