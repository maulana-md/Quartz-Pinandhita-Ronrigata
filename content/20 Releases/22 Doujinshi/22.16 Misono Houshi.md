## Commentary

This translation was commissioned by Anonymous.

More Mika goodies from Yamamoto, now with maid service. And more vanilla story to boot.

Was C104 after Blue Archive anime released? You can see that compared to [[22.12 Misono Kousoku]], Sensei this time is more like anime!Sensei, from appearance (hairdo and his full face) to personality, or at least I thought the scenario this time fits anime!Sensei better compared to Kousoku. Yandere Mika is good, but this, I like more.

With how he wrote the afterword and what's uploaded on e-h, it seems that he's defeated by Mika's charm, again. o7
