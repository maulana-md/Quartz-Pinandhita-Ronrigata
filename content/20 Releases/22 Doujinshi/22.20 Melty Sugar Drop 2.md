## Commentary

This is a special sequel for Melty Sugar Drop, the shorter doujinshi released by Shoa alongside [[22.04 Touhikou|Touhikou]] in C104 that we have translated, published in an omnibus of all Shoa's doujinshi until C105, *Sorairo Encore*.

I love seeing how a woman being corrupted in pleasure like this. What first started as merely fulfilling her estrus/"in heat" period, their activities continued on even after the fact. The plot is basically shoujo smut, isn't it?

Also, we missed Mari's birthday for a week or so. Happy late birthday, Mari!

We tried to give the dialogues more british accent touch, but it probably feels too half-assed. Apologies for that.
