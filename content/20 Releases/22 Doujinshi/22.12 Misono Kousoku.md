## Commentary

This translation was commissioned by Anonymous.

Not much to say about the story, it is (I think) the usual antics of "Mika did a bad thing > Need correction!" that a lot of Mika doujinka and artists do. Not to say it is bad, but it is nothing too remarkable. Still, it is enjoyable. The sudden switch of *"I need to correct Mika!!!"* in the middle of the book is a little too sudden and too cliche though.

In queue are other two Mika book from the same artist, alongside with a Kancolle book and some small Suisei galleries.
