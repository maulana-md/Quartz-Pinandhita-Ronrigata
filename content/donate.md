# Donation

This page contains details about our donation system and perks for doing so.

## Donation Perks

If you want to support us but unable to make a commission, you can (and should!) do that by donating to us.

The minimum donation amount is whatever is set by the platform (We set our Trakteer page to Rp 10.000 minimum, and ko-fi should have $5 minimum, while Paypal has none, I think), but as an incentive, if you donate at least $10 or Rp 100.000, you are eligible to add one doujinshi or H manga to our translation backlog, which can be seen [[backlog|here]]. Do note that 1) the same limitation for commissions still applies, and 2) doujinshi longer than 30-ish pages might be rejected if you only donate the minimal $10/Rp 100.000. Just donate more and those will not be a concern!

Another incentive that we have is for the overall donation amount, for every $30 raised on ko-fi and Paypal (will be counted separately for technical reason) and Rp 450.000 on Trakteer, we will add one more monthly translation release after that milestone is reached.

## How to Donate

You can give donation to us through several means.

### For Indonesians

You can donate to us through our [Trakteer page](https://trakteer.id/p.ronrigata). We're also looking to open other, more direct, means, like QRIS, in the future. Or alternatively, you can donate to our BCA account, just like paying for commissions.

### For People Overseas

You can donate to us through our [ko-fi page](https://ko-fi.com/pronrigata), or by sending money to our Paypal account with our contact email (p.ronrigata@gmail.com) or https://paypal.me/pronrigata.
